Introduction to the Yugo

The Yugo was a subcompact car produced by the Yugoslavian company Zastava Automobiles from 1980 to 2008. It was designed to be an affordable, fuel-efficient vehicle, and its low price point made it appealing to budget-conscious consumers. However, the Yugo's poor quality and numerous mechanical issues led to widespread criticism and a reputation for being one of the worst cars ever made.
