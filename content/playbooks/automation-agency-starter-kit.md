## Sample Excerpt

### The Automation Audit: Find $10K in Hidden Revenue for Any Client

Before you build a single automation, run this 45-minute audit with your client. It's the most valuable thing you'll ever do in a client engagement — and it's your best sales tool.

**Step 1: List every repetitive task.** Ask the client to walk through a typical week for every team member. Write down anything they do more than twice. The average SMB has 23 repetitive tasks that could be automated.

**Step 2: Score by impact.** For each task, calculate: hours per week × hourly cost × error rate. Sort descending. The top 5 tasks are your quick wins.

**Step 3: Map the data flow.** For each quick-win task, trace where the data starts, where it needs to go, and what transformations happen in between. This becomes your automation blueprint.

**Step 4: Present the ROI.** Build a simple spreadsheet showing: current monthly cost of doing it manually vs. your one-time setup fee + monthly retainer. The difference is your client's ROI — and your justification for pricing.

Most clients discover $3,000–$10,000/month in wasted labor during this audit. When you show them you can recover 70% of that for $1,500/month, the sale closes itself. This audit is also why you should never work on spec — the audit itself is worth your setup fee.
