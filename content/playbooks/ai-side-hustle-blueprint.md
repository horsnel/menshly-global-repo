## Sample Excerpt

### The 3-Hour Test: Validate Any AI Side Hustle Idea

Before you spend a weekend building anything, run this test. Pick your idea and spend exactly 3 hours on it:

**Hour 1: Research demand.** Search Reddit, Facebook Groups, and LinkedIn for people complaining about the problem your AI side hustle would solve. Count the posts. If you find fewer than 10 recent complaints, the market may be too small.

**Hour 2: Build a minimal offer.** Create a one-page landing page or a LinkedIn post describing your service. Don't build the AI system yet — just describe the outcome. "I'll automate your customer email responses using AI. $299/month setup, $99/month ongoing."

**Hour 3: Pitch 20 people.** Send 20 direct messages or emails to potential clients using the templates from Chapter 4. Track responses. If zero people express interest, pivot. If 2+ people ask for more details, you have signal.

Most people skip this test and spend weeks building something nobody wants. The 3-Hour Test saves you that wasted effort. Your goal isn't to prove your idea is brilliant — it's to find out if the market cares enough to pay.
