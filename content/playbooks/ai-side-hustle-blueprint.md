## What's Inside

This playbook is the distillation of 18 months of research into what actually works when building an AI-powered side hustle. Not theory. Not possibilities. Verified, repeatable business models that real people are using to generate income right now.

## The 25 Validated Models

Each model includes: the exact tools you need, the startup cost (most are under $50), the realistic first-month revenue expectation, and the step-by-step process to get your first paying customer. Models range from AI content agencies and chatbot building services to AI-powered data analysis consulting and automated lead generation systems.

## Pricing Templates

One of the biggest challenges for new side hustle operators is pricing. Charge too little and you can't sustain the business. Charge too much and you can't close deals. This playbook includes pricing templates for every model, based on real market data from over 200 freelancers and agencies. You'll know exactly what to charge, how to structure your packages, and when to raise your rates.

## The 30-Day Launch Plan

Day-by-day instructions for going from idea to first paying customer. Week one is setup and tool configuration. Week two is building your portfolio and outreach list. Week three is aggressive outreach and pitching. Week four is closing deals and delivering your first projects. Most people who follow this plan land their first client within 21 days.

## Who This Is For

This playbook is designed for people who want to earn $1,000 to $5,000 per month on the side using AI tools. You don't need coding experience. You don't need a large following. You need a computer, an internet connection, and the willingness to execute.
