## The Art and Science of Prompts

Prompt engineering isn't about clever tricks or secret formulas. It's about communicating clearly with a powerful but literal-minded assistant. This guide teaches you the systematic approach to crafting prompts that consistently produce high-quality outputs, regardless of the AI model you're using.

## The Framework

Every effective prompt follows the CRISP framework: Context (background information), Role (who the AI should be), Instructions (specific tasks), Specifications (format, length, tone), and Parameters (constraints and preferences). This guide walks you through each element with real examples and common pitfalls.

## 200+ Battle-Tested Prompts

Every prompt has been tested across ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini, with notes on which models perform best for each use case.

Beyond basic prompting, this guide covers chain-of-thought reasoning, few-shot learning, prompt chaining for complex workflows, and the art of system prompts that set the right foundation for extended conversations.
