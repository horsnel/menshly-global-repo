## What Nobody Warns You About

**1. The “Free” Tool Trap**  
You think you’re building an agency on a zero‑budget stack. Think again. Make.com’s free tier caps you at 100 operations/month—$49/month for 500. Zapier gives you 100 tasks free, then $19.99/month. Vapi charges $0.05/min for AI voice—run a 10‑minute client briefing and you’re already at $0.50. Hostinger’s cheapest plan is $3.99/month for 100 GB of storage. Spin those numbers up: 10 clients, 5 documents each, 5 min per doc. That’s $10/min × 10 × 5 = $500/month in voice costs alone. The “free” label is a sugarcoat, not a savings.

**2. The Compliance Grey Zone**  
Legal automation isn’t just code; it’s data. Storing draft contracts in Notion without HIPAA‑ready encryption costs an extra $10/month. If you pull clauses from LexisNexis, you’re paying $100/month for a limited API.
