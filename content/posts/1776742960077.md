For decades, scientists have been fascinated by the Neanderthals, seeking to understand their behavior, culture, and eventual demise. The discovery of the oldest Neanderthal group in the Stajnia Cave has provided a crucial piece of the puzzle, offering insights into the early history of this enigmatic species.</p>
