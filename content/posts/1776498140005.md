                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             <h2>Introduction to Advantech and NETINT Partnership</h2>
<p>Advantech, a leading provider of industrial computing solutions, has partnered with NETINT, a pioneering developer of video processing technology, to revolutionize the video infrastructure landscape. This strategic collaboration has yielded a groundbreaking solution that integrates NETINT's Codensity video processing units (VPUs) into Advantech's servers, resulting in unprecedented efficiency and scalability. The joint solution enables the processing of up to 384 live video streams in a compact 1RU server, setting a new benchmark for the industry.</p>

<h2>The Challenge of Video Infrastructure Scaling</h2>
<p>The exponential growth of video content has created a significant challenge for video infrastructure providers. As the demand for high-quality video streaming continues to rise, data centers and content delivery networks (CDNs) must scale their infrastructure to accommodate the increasing number of video streams. However, traditional central processing unit (CPU)-based solutions are often limited by their power consumption, heat generation, and processing capacity, making it difficult to achieve the required scalability. Furthermore, the complexity of video encoding and decoding processes has led to a surge in demand for specialized hardware accelerators that can efficiently handle these tasks.</p>

<h2>Advantech and NETINT's Innovative Solution</h2>
<p>The partnership between Advantech and NETINT addresses the scalability and efficiency challenges faced by video infrastructure providers. By leveraging NETINT's Codensity VPUs, Advantech's servers can now process multiple video streams in parallel, resulting in a significant increase in throughput and a reduction in power consumption. The Codensity VPU is a specialized hardware accelerator designed specifically for video encoding and decoding, providing a 10-20 times increase in performance compared to traditional CPU-based solutions. This enables Advantech's servers to support up to 384 live video streams in a single 1RU chassis, making it an ideal solution for data centers, CDNs, and video streaming services.</p>

<h2>Key Benefits of the Joint Solution</h2>
<p>The Advantech and NETINT joint solution offers several key benefits, including:</p>
<ul>
<li>Scalability: The solution enables the processing of up to 384 live video streams in a compact 1RU server, making it an ideal choice for large-scale video infrastructure deployments.</li>
<li>Efficiency: The use of NETINT's Codensity VPUs reduces power consumption and heat generation, resulting in lower operational costs and increased reliability.</li>
<li>Flexibility: The solution supports a wide range of video codecs, including H.264, H.265, and VP9, making it compatible with various video streaming applications.</li>
<li>Low Latency: The joint solution provides ultra-low latency, ensuring that video streams are delivered in real-time, making it suitable for applications such as live sports and online gaming.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Technical Specifications and Features</h2>
<p>The Advantech and NETINT joint solution is built on Advantech's VTA-7640 server, which features:</p>
<ul>
<li>Support for up to 4 Codensity VPUs, enabling the processing of up to 384 live video streams.</li>
<li>Intel Xeon Scalable processor for managing and controlling the VPUs.</li>
<li>Up to 256GB of DDR4 memory for storing video frames and metadata.</li>
<li>Dual 10GbE ports for connecting to the network and delivering video streams.</li>
<li>Redundant power supplies and fans for ensuring high reliability and uptime.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Real-World Applications and Use Cases</h2>
<p>The Advantech and NETINT joint solution has a wide range of real-world applications and use cases, including:</p>
<ul>
<li>Live video streaming: The solution is ideal for live sports, concerts, and other events, where low latency and high-quality video are crucial.</li>
<li>Cloud gaming: The joint solution can be used to deliver high-quality, low-latency video streams for cloud gaming applications.</li>
<li>Video surveillance: The solution can be used for video surveillance applications, such as monitoring public spaces, transportation hubs, and commercial buildings.</li>
<li>Social media and online platforms: The joint solution can be used to deliver high-quality video streams for social media and online platforms, such as YouTube, Facebook, and Twitch.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Conclusion and Future Outlook</h2>
<p>In conclusion, the partnership between Advantech and NETINT has resulted in a groundbreaking solution that brings VPU-powered efficiency to video infrastructure. The joint solution enables the processing of up to 384 live video streams in a compact 1RU server, making it an ideal choice for data centers, CDNs, and video streaming services. As the demand for high-quality video streaming continues to rise, the Advantech and NETINT joint solution is poised to play a significant role in shaping the future of video infrastructure. With its unparalleled scalability, efficiency, and flexibility, the solution is expected to have a major impact on the industry, enabling the widespread adoption of high-quality video streaming and paving the way for new and innovative applications.</p>
